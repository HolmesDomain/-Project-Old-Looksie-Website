International Love


We ship worldwide. Orders will be processed 24 hours or less after checkout. Once processed you will receive a confirmation and a shipment alert via email. We keep you informed! We do not ship on Saturday, Sunday, or Holidays. Shipping rates and delivery times will differ according to the shipping service used (UPS, USPS for example), packages usually arrive between 3-7 days. Looksie operates and mails from the East coast of the US, which means international delivery time will vary widely. We'll make sure to get your order out as soon as possible! Unfortunatly at this time package tracking is unavailable. ✈️

If your package is mishandled or damaged upon delivery, please first contact your local delivery service. However, if assistance is still needed after, contact us about your order.
